# Content idea card

One file per idea. Firm content → `Projects/{content-project}/ideas/`;
client content → `Clients/{slug}/work/{deliverable}/ideas/` (from client scope).
Frontmatter when instantiated: `type: note`, `status: draft|ready|published|killed`,
`tags: [content, idea]`.

## Hook
The one line that earns the click.

## Audience + trigger
Who this is for, and what just happened to them.

## Proof
The number, story, or artifact that makes it credible. No proof, no draft.

## Format
Post / newsletter / video / page.
