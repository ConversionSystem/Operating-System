# Competitor profile skeleton

For `Company/market/{competitor-slug}.md` (our market) or a client's
`context/market.md` entries (their market — write it from client scope).
Frontmatter when instantiated: `type: context`, `reviewed:`, `tags: [market, competitor]`.

## Who they are
One paragraph: what they sell, to whom, at what price if known.

## Their pitch
Positioning and the promise, paraphrased — link sources.

## Strengths
One per line, each citing evidence (a page, a review, a lost-deal note).

## Weak spots
Same rule: no evidence, no entry.

## What we do about it
The talk track: when we win against them, when we lose, what to say.

## Watch
Signals worth rechecking and when. Update on contact with reality
(lost deal, prospect mention) — not on a calendar.
