# Template — client brief (internal, one screen)

The strategist's get-up-to-speed read: built from profile, goals ledger
(last 5 lines per goal), decisions log, last 3 meetings, and open work
briefs. Not saved unless asked — it's a read, not a record.

## State of play

One paragraph: scope, status, money, mood. A new teammate should know
whether to relax or brace after reading it.

## Momentum

The last 3 ledger moves and the last meeting's outcome — what's actually
moving, with dates.

## Risks

Tripped health signals only, each citing its evidence file. No vibes.

## Open loops

Unchecked actions with ages and owners, oldest first.

## Recommended next actions

Exactly 3, each concrete: verb, object, deadline.
