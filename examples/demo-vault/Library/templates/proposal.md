# Template — proposal

One page beats ten. Their words for the problem, our words for the plan,
numbers everywhere a number exists.

## The situation

The prospect's problem in their own words — quote what they said in the
sales conversations, don't paraphrase it into agency-speak.

## What success looks like

Measurable outcomes with baselines and targets. If we can't measure it,
it doesn't go here.

## The plan

Phases with what happens when: week ranges, the work in each, what they'll
see shipped. No methodology theater.

## Investment

Price from Company/offers.md patterns — flat retainer, what's included,
the floor conditions. Never quote from memory.

## Why us

Positioning plus proof points (from Company/brand.md): who we've been, what
we've held flat or moved, how we report.

## Next step

One action, one date. A proposal that ends with options ends nowhere.
