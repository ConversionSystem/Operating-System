# Template — playbook

How we deliver a service, written so a competent new hire could run it in
week one. One playbook per offer in Company/offers.md.

## When to run this

The trigger conditions: engagement type, account state, signals that this
is the right play.

## Inputs

What must exist before step 1 — access, data exports, briefs, approvals.
Missing inputs are a blocker to raise, not a thing to improvise around.

## Steps

Numbered. Each step carries an owner role and a quality bar — the check
that says the step is actually done, not just attempted.

## Outputs

The artifacts and states this playbook leaves behind, including what gets
logged to the goals ledger.

## Common failure modes

The ways this play goes wrong in practice, and the tell for each.

## Examples

Anonymized only — patterns and numbers with the names filed off. Client
material never enters the Library.
