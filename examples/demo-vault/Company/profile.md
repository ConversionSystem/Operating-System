# Brightline Growth — profile

## Identity

Brightline Growth is a 3-person growth agency founded in 2024 by Maya Chen,
run remote from Seattle. We do two things for DTC consumer brands between
$2M and $20M in revenue: paid acquisition (Meta + Google) and lifecycle
email/SMS (Klaviyo). One-liner, in Maya's words: "We make every ad dollar
show its receipt."

Roster: Maya Chen (founder — strategy, paid media, all client relationships),
Jonah Reyes (growth marketer — lifecycle email, campaign execution), Tessa Liu
(contract designer, ~15 hrs/week — ad creative and email design, no vault seat).

## How we operate

- Small book, deep work: max 6 retainer clients, ever. We renew on results.
- Every metric claim traces to a goals ledger line. No screenshots-as-proof.
- Decisions get written down the day they're made, with the reasoning.
- Weekly client reports go out Monday by 12:00 PT, generated from the ledger.
- Async-first: Slack for same-day, the vault for everything durable. Meetings
  are 30 minutes, agenda-first, decisions extracted same day.

## What a client experiences

A weekly rhythm they can set their watch to: Monday report with real numbers,
one working call, and a team that says "here's what we'd cut" before "here's
what we'd add". They see CAC, ROAS, and email revenue share move — and when a
number moves the wrong way, we flag it before they ask.

June 2026: one anchor A-tier retainer in month four, renewal decision lands

Maya's selling time. The push this quarter is proving the anchor account
