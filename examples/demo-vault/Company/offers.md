# Offers — what we sell

## Paid Acquisition (paid-acquisition)

Full management of Meta + Google paid programs for DTC brands.

- For: brands spending $20k–$150k/month on ads whose blended CAC is drifting up.
- Price: $4,500/month retainer, flat — no percent-of-spend, ever.
- Delivery: account restructure in weeks 1–3, then a weekly cycle of creative
  testing (3–5 new assets/week from Tessa), budget reallocation, and a Monday
  report with ROAS/CAC straight from the ledger.
- Timeline: 90 days to a defensible CAC trend; restructures show in weeks 2–4.

## Lifecycle Email & SMS (lifecycle-email)

Klaviyo flows and campaign program, owned end to end.

- For: brands with >25k list size whose email is under 20% of revenue.
- Price: $3,000/month retainer. SMS included once list passes 10k opted-in.
- Delivery: core-four flows rebuilt first (welcome, abandon, post-purchase,
  winback), then 2–3 campaigns/week written in the client's voice, with
  deliverability checks monthly.
- Timeline: flows live within 30 days; email revenue share moves in 60–90.

## How we price

Flat retainers only. Floor is $2,500/month for any single service; both
services together start at $7,500/month. Price moves with channel count,
creative volume, and SKU complexity — never with ad spend, so our incentive
stays on efficiency. Quarterly commitments, 30-day out clause after month 3.

## What we don't do

- SEO, site builds, or dev work — we refer out.
- Organic social management.
- One-off audits without an implementation path (they shelf-ware).
- Performance-only ("pay us from upside") deals — misaligned on data access.
- Brands under $1M revenue — they need a generalist, not us.
