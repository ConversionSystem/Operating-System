# Health engine + generated roll-ups

Mechanical, evidence-cited risk scoring. No vibes: every flag names its file.

## Signals (evaluate all; each tripped signal is cited in health-note)

| # | Signal | Trip condition | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| S0 | Flying blind | any of cadence / last-meeting / goals.md / renewal missing on an active client ΓÇö the signals below can't all be evaluated | profile.md |
| S1 | Gone quiet | days since `last-meeting` > 2├ù cadence period | profile.md |
| S2 | Stale data | newest ledger line older than 14 days (active clients) | goals.md |
| S3 | Metric regression | any goal's last 3 observations moving against `direction` | goals.md ledger |
| S4 | Renewal exposure | `renewal:` Γëñ 45 days AND (health Γëá green OR any goal < 50% to target) | profile.md + goals.md |
| S5 | Rotting actions | unchecked `- [ ]` older than 14 days in last 3 meeting notes | meetings/ |
| S6 | Sentiment drift | dissatisfaction phrasing in ANY of the last 2 meetings ("disappointed", "we expected", "by now", "escalate", "reconsider") | meetings/ |
| S7 | Unpriced work | `mrr: 0` with `status: active` | profile.md |
| S8 | Review overdue | `last-review` older than 30 days | profile.md |

## Scoring

- 0 signals ΓåÆ `green` ┬╖ 1ΓÇô2 ΓåÆ `yellow` ┬╖ ΓëÑ3 OR (S4 + any other) ΓåÆ `red`.
- S0 caps health at `yellow`: an uninstrumented client can never be green ΓÇö
  the health-note must name the missing inputs ("S0: no goals.md, no renewal
  date ΓÇö instrument before trusting this row").
- Write `health`, `health-note` ("S1: 19d since last meeting (cadence weekly); S3:
  cpl rising 3 obs"), `last-review: {{TODAY}}` to profile.md.
- Health NEVER improves silently: green requires zero signals on a fresh
  evaluation, not the absence of a recent check.
